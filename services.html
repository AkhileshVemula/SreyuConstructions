Our Construction Services

Residential Construction

We build custom homes, villas, and apartments with a focus on quality, aesthetics, and Vastu compliance. From foundation to finishing, we ensure your dream home is built to last.

Commercial Construction

Our team delivers state-of-the-art commercial spaces, including offices, retail centers, and warehouses, designed for functionality and modern appeal.

Renovations & Remodeling

We transform existing spaces with expert renovations, including structural upgrades, interior redesigns, and energy-efficient solutions.

Interior Design

Our turnkey interior solutions include modular kitchens, wardrobes, false ceilings, and Vastu-friendly layouts for stunning and practical spaces.

Architectural Design

We create detailed 2D/3D designs and structural drawings, ensuring innovative, functional, and aesthetically pleasing plans.

Project Management

Our expert project managers oversee every phase, ensuring timely delivery, budget adherence, and seamless coordination.

Our 6-Phase Construction Process

1. Initiation

Site visits, feasibility studies, and client consultations to define project goals, budget, and Vastu requirements.

2. Design

2D/3D designs, structural drawings, and electrical/plumbing plans for a cohesive and innovative blueprint.

3. Preconstruction

Permits, cost estimation, and scheduling to ensure a smooth transition to the construction phase.

4. Procurement

Sourcing high-quality materials like cement, steel, and tiles with rigorous quality checks.

5. Construction

Executing RCC work, roofing, plumbing, and finishing with regular updates and safety standards.

6. Post-Construction

Final inspections, cleaning, and interior styling for a move-in-ready space with warranty support.

Sreyu Constructions & Engineering delivers innovative and sustainable construction solutions, transforming visions into reality with precision and passion.
