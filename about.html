About Sreyu Constructions

Building Trust, One Project at a Time

Founded in 2015 by Ganapuram Vamshi, Sreyu Constructions & Engineering is a leading construction firm based in Nizamabad, Telangana. With a decade of experience, we specialize in residential, commercial, and interior design projects, delivering spaces that combine innovation, durability, and aesthetic appeal. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in construction.

Our Vision

To be the premier construction company in India, known for innovative designs, sustainable practices, and exceptional client experiences.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality construction and design solutions that exceed expectations, blending modern technology with traditional craftsmanship.

  • Sustainability
